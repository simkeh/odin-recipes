Loaded Sweet potato

Description

These are the best sweet potatos you will ever have!

Ingredients

  • 1-2 Sweet potatoes
  • A couple slices of Cheddar or Monterney Jack cheese
  • 2 Slices of Bacon
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. You start out with 2 medium sized sweet potatoes per person.
  2. Make slices (halfway through) in it with a distance of 1/8th in.
  3. Preheat the oven on 350 and leave them in there for one hour.
  4. While theyre baking, cut the bacon and Cheese in little slices.
  5. Once theyre ready, stuff the bacon and cheese in the slots
  6. bake for another 10mins with the remainder of the heat int he turned off oven
  7. Enjoy!
